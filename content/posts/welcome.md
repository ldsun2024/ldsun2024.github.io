## Hello, World.

This is the first entry in what I hope will become a quiet corner of the internet — a space for thoughts on writing, literature, and the creative process.

### What to expect

This blog will document my journey as a writer, including:

- **The Craft** — notes on storytelling, structure, and the writing process
- **Behind the Scenes** — glimpses into my novel projects and the creative workflow
- **Reflections** — thoughts on technology, reading, and life

### About the Literature-Wiki project

I've been developing a structured approach to novel writing — a five-stage, eight-step creative paradigm that moves from concept to completed manuscript. It's a system I call the **Creation Work Paradigm**, and pieces of it will find their way into these pages.
